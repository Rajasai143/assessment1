About us


Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) is established in 1997 by
Dr. G Gangaraju as a self-financed institute under the aegis of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society.
GRIET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, permanently affiliated to and autonomous under JNTUH, Hyderabad.
GRIET is committed to quality education and is known for its innovative teaching practices.
